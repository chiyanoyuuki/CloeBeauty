Behind the brush

Welcome to my website and thank you for being here. This is the corner of the website to know more about me. So, a little bit about my journey... I was born and raised in Paris, where romance intertwines effortlessly with style and luxury. After graduating international marketing and working in digital communication, I decided in 2019 to follow my true passion and became a bridal makeup artist and hairstylist.

I love drawing inspiration from the timeless elegance of the iconic city of Paris, where the art of beauty is celebrated in every detail, and from the women who surround me: my beautiful mother, my sisters and every bride-to-be who trusts me with their beauty. Their grace, strength and love fuel my passion for creating exquisite bridal looks. My experience as a sales associate for high-end brands such as Céline and Courrèges also allowed me to develop my sense of perfection and detail and confirmed my passion for French luxury.

Over the years, I had the honor to be a part of more than 200 stunning celebrations and to see my work published on wedding publications such as Style Me Pretty, Madame Figaro, Wedding Sparrow, Wedluxe and Magnolia Rouge. ​

My approach

My signature style

Your wedding day should celebrate you; a version of yourself that feels authentic, radiant, and unforgettable. My signature style is all about creating modern and effortlessly elegant looks that complement my brides style and beauty features. I focus on soft and luminous finishes to ensure a flawless yet natural complexion, without feeling overdone. Imagine the refined bridal beauty of Sofia Richie, Olivia Culpo and Hailey Bieber; luminous and modern with a natural glow that exudes sophistication. When it comes to hair, I aim for effortless styles that stand the test of time. Whether it’s soft waves, delicate updos, or chic and undone textures, my goal is to create hairstyles that feel romantic and timeless, complementing your natural beauty and lasting all day long.

A tailored experience

My intention for you is to have an unforgettably simple and tailored experience from the moment you contact me until the moment you walk down the aisle, ensuring every detail is meticulously planned and executed. My goal is to provide all the time and attention you need and to make you feel perfectly polished and confident on such a memorable day in your life.​​​

An exclusive service

I believe every bride deserves my full attention and the highest level of service on her special day. To ensure I can give each bride the best possible experience, I take on a limited number of weddings per year and I only commit to one wedding per day. Your wedding day is a once-in-a-lifetime moment, and I am here to make sure you feel absolutely stunning from start to finish.
